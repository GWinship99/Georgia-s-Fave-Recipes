Tiramisu

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

400ml double cream

250g mascarpone

75ml marsala wine

5 tbsp golden caster sugar

300ml coffee which is made from 2 tbsp coffee granules and 300ml boiling water

175g sponge fingers

25g dark chocolate

2tsp cocoa powder

Instructions

1. Put the double cream, mascarpone, marsala wine and golden caster sugar in a large bowl.

2. Whisk until the cream and mascarpone have completely combined and have the consistency of thickly whipped cream.

3. Pour the coffee into a shallow dish. Dip in a few of the sponge fingers at a time, turning for a few seconds until they are nicely soaked, but not soggy. Layer these in a dish until you've used half the sponge fingers, then spread over half of the creamy mixture.

4. Using the coarse side of the grater, grate over most of the dark chocolate. Then repeat the layers (use up all the coffee), finishing with the creamy layer.

5. Cover and chill for a few hours or overnight. Make sure to eat before two days have elapsed of it being in the fridge.

6. To serve, dust the tiramisu with the cocoa powder and grate over the remainder of the chocolate.
