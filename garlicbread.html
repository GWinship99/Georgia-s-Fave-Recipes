Garlic Bread

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Minutes: 25 minutes

Introduction

Garlic bread has always been one of my favourite starters and sides to order at a restaurant or eat at home. I just love the crunchy texture combined with the fragrant herby flavour of the garlic bread. Plus garlic bread can in itself be a blank canvas as you can top it with essentially anything including cheese, tomato, maybe a bit of pesto too!

Ingredients

1 Loaf of French/Italian Bread

6 tbsp Butter

2 Tbsp Olive Oil

4 cloves of minced garlic

2 tbsp chopped Parsley

1/8 tsp Salt

1/4 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. In a bowl, stir together the room temperature butter, olive oil, minced garlic, garlic powder, chopped parsley and salt until relatively smooth (a few small lumps of butter is fine).

2. Cut the loaf of bread in half, into two 12 inch long pieces, then cut each piece open lengthwise. Lay the bread on a baking sheet cut sides facing up. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the open surfaces of the bread.

3. Bake the bread for 10-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and crispy. Bake for less time if you prefer a softer bread or bake for longer if you like it extra crispy. Cut the bread into 2 inch sections and serve hot. Enjoy!
