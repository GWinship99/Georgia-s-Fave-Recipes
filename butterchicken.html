Butter Chicken

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Total Minutes: 35 minutes

Introduction

One of my go to orders that I love to get either from an Indian restaurant or from the Indian section at my local supermarket is this amazing, moreish, silky smooth Indian delicacy. I just love the taste and texture of this amazing dish so of course I had to include it in my book

Ingredients

0.17 cup of full fat plain yoghurt

0.33 tbsp lemon juice

0.33 tsp turmeric powder

0.67 tsp garam masala

0.17 tsp chilli powder or cayenne pepper powder

0.33 tsp ground cumin

0.33 tbsp ginger, finely grated

0.67 cloves of garlic, crushed

250g chicken thighs, cut into bite size pieces

10g ghee or butter

0.33 cup of tomato passata

0.33 cup of heavy thickened cream

0.33 tbsp sugar

0.42 tsp salt

White or basmati rice

Instructions

1. Bonus prep step: combine the ingredients for the marinade in a food processor and blend until smooth

2. Combine the marinade ingredients with the chicken in a bowl. Cover up and refridgerate overnight.

3. Heat the ghee over high heat in a large frying pan. Take the chicken out of the marinade but don't wipe the marinade off the chicken and also don't pour the marinade in the pan.

4. Place the chicken in the frying pan and cook for around 3 minutes, or until the chicken is white all over.

5. Add the tomato passata, cream, sugar and salt. Also add the remaining marinade from the bowl and turn the heat down to low so it can simmer for 20 minutes. Add more salt to taste if need be.

6. Garnish with coriander or cilantro and serve the now cook curry with your choice of rice.

7. If you want some sides with your curry,I recommend naan bread and poppadoms!
