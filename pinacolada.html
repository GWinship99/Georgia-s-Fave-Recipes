Pina Colada

Total Minutes: 5 minutes

Introduction

This cocktail is the personification of summer in a glass. Tropical, cocunutty and finally that classic rum kick. If you want to imagine you're in paradise, this is the drink for you. Enjoy this quick and easy recipe!

Ingredients

120ml pineapple juice

60ml Malibu rum

60ml coconut cream

(optional) wedge of pineapple to garnish

Instructions

1. Pulse all the ingredients along with a handful of ice ina blender until smooth. Pour into a tall glass and garnish as you like.
